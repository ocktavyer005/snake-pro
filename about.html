🐍 Snake Pro

Snake Pro is a modern HTML5 snake game created for players who enjoy fast, simple, and exciting gameplay. The game is optimized for smartphones, tablets, and desktop devices, delivering smooth performance directly in your web browser without requiring installation.

Game Features

  • Modern and responsive gameplay
  • Easy touch and keyboard controls
  • Bonus food system
  • High score leaderboard
  • Multiple snake skins
  • Fast loading and lightweight design
  • Installable as a Progressive Web App (PWA)

How to Play

Control the snake, collect food to increase your score, avoid colliding with yourself, and challenge yourself to achieve the highest score possible. The longer you survive, the greater your challenge becomes.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide a fun, free, and enjoyable snake game that can be played by everyone, anywhere, on any device. We are committed to continuously improving the game by adding new features, better performance, and an even more enjoyable gaming experience.
